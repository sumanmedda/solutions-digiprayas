Beginning of a Dreamer

I was 13 years old when I posted my first video online - coming from a tiny town in Haryana, this was a big deal.I was thrilled by the 26,000 views I got on the very first video that I posted online, and then...

Becoming a Creator

I went on to build multiple YouTube channels and an internet company.
As 13-14-year-olds trying to run a company, we faced various challenges, and finally, I had to put work on hold to focus on the IIT entrance exam.

Finally, I chose to attend BITS Pilani where I discovered my love for entrepreneurship.

But it wasn't enough...

Freelancing & Filmmaking

I started freelancing in video editing and film direction, working for big names in the industry, working in movies, advertising, tv shows etc. My campaigns and videos were aired on various global platforms.

But, it got tiring, and that's when I started Motion Trooper, my first (failed) agency. When it didn't work, I experienced one of the lowest phases..

Finding Purpose

While the world was going through the COVID reality check, I founded OWLED - from the same room where I made my first-ever video.

OWLED went on to become bigger than I could ever imagine. It started with a cold email to Ankur Warikoo in the middle of the night.

OWLED is now a creative powerhouse helping brands like Disney+, CRED, PhonePe, Estee Lauder, HP with videos, marketing, content, design, AR tech and a lot more

Evolving and Expanding

Since 2021, OWLED has soared, growing into a 50+ member team working with international brands. Our acclaimed work has firmly established us in the industry.

Beyond digital, we've launched an innovative offline marketing school, collaborating with industry leaders. This phase marks not just business growth but a legacy of creativity, innovation, and impact, as we continue to invest in people and ideas shaping the future.
